By Admin

Description:

Under general guidance, develops marketing plans and strategies for the Division, and for individual business segments. Develops a Division strategy and ensures the approach is synchronized with overall company strategy. This role will lead all promotion strategy sessions as well as lead ideation sessions. Ensures cross divisional promotions meet necessary criteria for each segment. Assists in new sales and client retention initiatives.Sodexo is currently searching for a Director, Creative Services - Knowledge Management.
